## What Makes It Hard

- 4 tones (+ neutral) — same syllable, completely different meaning
- Characters must be memorized, no alphabet shortcut
- ~2,000 chars to read casually, ~3,500 for basic literacy
- No grammatical overlap with English or Spanish

Upside: grammar is simpler than Spanish. No conjugation, no gendered nouns.

---

## Resources Worth Knowing

- **Anki** — spaced repetition, best for character and vocab retention
- **Pleco** — best Chinese dictionary app, has flashcard mode
- **HSK lists** — standardized vocab levels (HSK 1 = ~150 words → HSK 6 = ~5,000)
- **HelloChinese** — decent for structure early on
- **Comprehensible input** — listening/reading slightly above your level is the fastest way up
