## Maintenance

*(fill in: oil, tires, brakes, fluids, filters, timing belt/chain, coolant, battery — what each is, when to change, what happens if you don't)*

| Item | What it does | Service interval | What happens if ignored |
|---|---|---|---|
| Engine oil | Lubricates moving parts, carries heat away | Every 5,000–10,000 miles (synthetic) | Engine seizes |
| Tire pressure | Affects handling, fuel economy, wear | Check monthly | Uneven wear, blowout risk |
| Brake pads | Friction material that stops the car | ~30,000–70,000 miles depending on driving | Metal-on-metal grinding, brake failure |
| Air filter | Filters air to engine | ~15,000–30,000 miles | Reduced power, worse fuel economy |
| Cabin air filter | Filters air to interior | ~15,000–25,000 miles | Smells, reduced HVAC airflow |
| Coolant | Regulates engine temperature | ~30,000 miles or every 2 years | Overheating, head gasket failure |
| Battery | Powers all electrical systems | 3–5 year lifespan | Won't start |

## Components & Their Purpose

*(fill in: engine types, transmission types, suspension, braking systems, drivetrain: FWD/RWD/AWD/4WD, differentials, cooling, fuel system, exhaust)*

| Component | Purpose | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Differential | Allows wheels on same axle to spin at different speeds in turns | Open diff vs. limited slip (LSD) vs. locked |
| Catalytic converter | Converts exhaust pollutants to less harmful gases | Expensive because of platinum-group metals inside |
| Turbocharger | Compresses intake air for more power | Uses exhaust gas to spin a turbine. Turbo lag = delay before boost builds. |
| Intercooler | Cools compressed air before it enters engine | Denser cool air = more oxygen = more power |

## Drivetrain

| Type | What it means | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| FWD | Front wheels drive + steer | Efficiency, packaging, everyday driving |
| RWD | Rear wheels drive | Performance, balance, fun |
| AWD | All wheels driven, automatic distribution | Wet/snow traction, performance |
| 4WD | Selectable 4-wheel drive, usually with low range | Off-road |
