Social and party games worth knowing.

| Game | Players | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| **The Chameleon** | 3–8 | One player is secretly the Chameleon — doesn't know the secret word. Everyone gives a one-word clue related to it. Chameleon tries to blend in. Others try to find them. |
| **Codenames** | 4+ (teams) | Two teams. Spymasters give one-word clues to get teammates to guess the right words on the board without hitting the assassin. Language + deduction. |
| **Throw Throw Burrito** | 2–6 | Combination of card matching and dodgeball — collect matching cards, but some cards trigger foam burrito throwing mid-game. Chaotic. |
| **Exploding Kittens** | 2–5 | Draw cards until someone draws an exploding kitten (and loses). Use action cards to dodge, defuse, and sabotage. Designed by The Oatmeal. |
| **Poker — Texas Hold'em** | 2–10 | Each player gets 2 hole cards, 5 community cards are dealt face up in stages (flop, turn, river). Best 5-card hand wins the pot. |

---

## Poker Quick Reference (Texas Hold'em)

**Hand rankings (high to low):**
Royal Flush → Straight Flush → Four of a Kind → Full House → Flush → Straight → Three of a Kind → Two Pair → Pair → High Card

**Betting rounds:** Pre-flop → Flop (3 cards) → Turn (1 card) → River (1 card)

**Positions matter:** Dealer (button) acts last post-flop — biggest positional advantage.
