## David Gemmell

Soldier, warrior, and heroic fiction author. Writes about ordinary people choosing courage.

> *"We make choices everyday, some of them good, some of them bad. And if we are strong enough, we live with the consequences."*

> *"I may be stupid, as you say, to believe in honour and generosity, but at least I do not have to wake each day and face a pitiless world alone."*
