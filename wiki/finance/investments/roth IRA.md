## Why It's Interesting

Right in the mechanical engineer wheelhouse — gearing, torque, blade pitch. Could be a desktop piece, a functional tool, or both.

---

## Possible Approaches

- **Gear-driven:** hand crank → gear train → fan blade. Ratio determines speed/effort tradeoff
- **Friction drive:** simpler, less efficient but fewer parts
- Enclosure: wood, 3D printed, or machined
