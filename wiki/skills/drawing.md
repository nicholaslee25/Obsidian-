## Progression Path (rough)

1. Lines + basic shapes (comfort with the pencil)
2. Shading — hatching, cross-hatching, blending
3. Simple objects from observation
4. Human figure basics (gesture, proportion)
5. Perspective (1-pt, 2-pt)
6. Style development
