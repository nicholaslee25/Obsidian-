## Framework

Not high / medium / low — that collapses urgency and motivation into one axis and isn't useful. Instead:

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🔴 **Now** | Active, doing it, time-sensitive or high momentum |
| 🟡 **Soon** | Important, needs scheduling, stalled or not started |
| 🟢 **Queue** | Wants to do, no hard deadline, waits for right timing or mood |
| ⚪ **Someday** | Cool idea, no timeline, can wait indefinitely |
