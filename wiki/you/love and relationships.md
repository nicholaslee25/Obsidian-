## Media That Hit Different

### Love of Kill (ch. 41) + Call of the Night

Filed during the aftermath of the breakup with D.

Both of these carry the same feeling: *loving and being loved without the other person knowing.* That unnamed, unreciprocated current that runs between people — the weight of it, and the quiet ache of it being invisible.

> Reminder to yourself: you experience this on **both ends.** You've been the one who didn't know, and the one who knew and said nothing.

What these also said, clearly: **life goes on with or without you.** Everyone's circumstances are different. Everyone's way of coping is different. Everyone's preferences and timelines are different. None of that is a verdict on you.

Don't need a relationship to prove something. Don't need closure to move. Keep that close.
