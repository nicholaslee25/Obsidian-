# Pole Dropper Reflex Machine

**Type:** Project
**Tags:** #project #electronics #game #reaction-time
**Status:** Idea

---

## Overview

A reflex-testing machine based on the classic "ruler drop" test — a pole or rod drops at a random time and the player catches or reacts to it as fast as possible. Measures reaction time.

## How It Might Work

- Electromagnet or servo holds the pole at the top
- Random delay timer (microcontroller) releases it
- Sensor at the bottom (or catch position) measures the time from release to reaction
- Display shows reaction time in milliseconds

## Components (Rough)

- Pole/rod (wooden or metal)
- Electromagnet or servo release mechanism
- Microcontroller (Arduino/Teensy)
- Random delay logic
- Reaction sensor (touch, light break, or button)
- Display (7-segment or OLED)

## Open Questions
