## Overview

An NFC tag that, when tapped with a phone, automatically connects it to a WiFi network — no typing passwords, no QR codes. Tap and you're on. Android natively supported; iPhone works via iOS 14+. Relatively quick build, high payoff.

## Materials

- NFC tags (NTAG213 or NTAG215, ~$5–10 for a pack)
- NFC Tools app (free) to write WiFi credentials to the tag
- Optional enclosure: wood coaster, 3D printed wall plate, or embedded in a desk piece
