# Custom Chessboard

**Type:** Project
**Tags:** #project #woodworking #games
**Last updated:** 2026-06-01

---

## Concept

Handmade wooden chessboard. Could be a standalone display piece or functional set with storage.

---

## Design Options

**Board Construction**
- Classic 8×8 alternating light/dark squares — maple (light) + walnut (dark) is the standard combo
- Method: glue alternating strips, cross-cut at 90°, re-glue alternating to form checkerboard pattern
- Border frame (optional): contrasting wood or same species with a routed edge profile

**Storage**
- Drawer built into the base for pieces
- Hinged board that opens like a box — pieces store inside
- Separate box with compartments

**Square Size**
- Standard: 57mm (2¼ in) squares, 57mm border
- Compact: 45–50mm for a smaller board

**Pieces**
- Buy turned wood pieces (inexpensive, finishable)
- Buy resin or metal pieces
- Make your own if you have a lathe — a future skill

**Extras**
- Felt base to protect surfaces
- CNC-engraved coordinates (a1–h8) in the border
- Burned or etched rank/file notation
- Personalized text or crest
