## Overview

Using NFC to trigger or communicate with a microcontroller — tap a tag or phone to a reader and something happens: a device toggles, a mode switches, a display updates. NFC as a "key" or control interface.

## Materials

- NFC reader module (PN532 — I2C, SPI, or UART)
- Microcontroller (ESP32 or Arduino)
- NFC tags or use phone as the tag
- Whatever the MCU controls (relay, LEDs, display, etc.)
- Power supply and wiring
