**Type:** Project
**Tags:** #project #cars #electronics #diy
**Status:** Idea

---

## Overview

Replace or augment the car's factory washer spray motors with a much higher-pressure system. Add a dedicated button + routed tube that fires wiper fluid at a tailgater's windshield. Harmless — small volume, just water.

## Approach Options

- **Upgrade factory pump** — swap OEM motor with a higher-PSI pump on the same connector
- **Add secondary system** — separate reservoir + pump + switch + tube routed to fire rearward; fully independent of the factory setup
- **Nozzle redirect** — reroute or re-aim an existing nozzle; lower effort but less satisfying
