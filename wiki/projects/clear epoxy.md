## Overview

Get into clear epoxy casting. Research the process, order supplies, start experimenting.

## Research Checklist

- [ ] Mold types — silicone, plastic, wood forms, custom-made
- [ ] Layer depth limits — most epoxy max pour is ¼"–½" per layer (heat buildup if too thick)
- [ ] Temperature — ambient vs. heat-cured; warmer = faster cure, can cause bubbles if too hot
- [ ] Cure time — working time (minutes) vs. full cure (typically 24–72h depending on product)
- [ ] Cost — resin + hardener ratio (typically 1:1 or 2:1 by volume), cost per pour
- [ ] Brands — Total Boat, ArtResin, Pro Marine Supplies, Stone Coat
