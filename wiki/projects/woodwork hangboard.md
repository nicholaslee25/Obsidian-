## Overview

A handmade wooden hangboard (fingerboard) for climbing training. Wall-mounted, used for dead hangs, finger strength, and grip work.

## Design Considerations

- **Wood type** — hardwood preferred (oak, maple, ash); needs to handle repeated load without splinting
- **Edges** — variety of depths (20mm, 15mm, 10mm crimps), slopers, pinches, jugs
- **Surface finish** — light sanding only; too smooth = no friction, too rough = skin tear
- **Mount** — wall studs or a freestanding frame; needs to handle bodyweight + dynamic load
- **Ergonomics** — hand spacing, edge angles, wrist position
