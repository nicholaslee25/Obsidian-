## Overview

An instrument or musical device that uses NFC — either NFC tags that trigger sounds/samples when tapped, or an NFC-based interface for a physical instrument. Each tag tap triggers a specific note, sample, or effect.

## Materials

- NFC reader module (PN532 — I2C/SPI)
- NFC tags (NTAG213 or 215)
- Microcontroller (ESP32 or RP2040)
- Audio output (DAC + amp, or I2S speaker board)
- Enclosure (wood, acrylic, or 3D printed surface)
- Wiring and power supply
