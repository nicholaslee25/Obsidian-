## Things to Learn First

- Battery chemistry basics (Li-ion, NiMH, lead-acid — what's different)
- How capacity is measured: **mAh** (milliamp-hours) — a 2000mAh battery can deliver 2A for 1 hour, or 1A for 2 hours
- **C-rate** — charge/discharge rate as a multiple of capacity. 1C = full charge in 1 hour. 0.5C = 2 hours. Most Li-ion charged at 0.5–1C.
- **CC/CV charging** — Constant Current until near-full, then Constant Voltage to top off. Standard for Li-ion.
- Cell sizes: 18650, 21700, AA, AAA — just different physical formats
