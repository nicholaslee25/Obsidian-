# Custom USB Stick Chassis

**Type:** Project
**Tags:** #project #woodworking #electronics #design
**Last updated:** 2026-06-01

---

## Concept

Replace the stock plastic casing of a USB drive with a custom-made chassis. The drive guts stay the same — only the shell changes.

---

## Material Options

| Material | Look | Difficulty | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Wood** | Warm, natural | Low–medium | Easy to shape; sand + oil finish; risk: thin walls may crack |
| **Resin** | Translucent, glowy | Medium | Cast around the bare PCB; can embed objects inside |
| **Metal (aluminum)** | Clean, machined | High | Needs milling or lathe; premium feel; harder to do by hand |
| **3D printed** | Custom shape | Low | Fast iteration; PLA/resin; can combine with wood inlay |

---

## Design Considerations

- Bare USB PCBs are tiny — look up dimensions for the specific drive before building
- Retention: the PCB needs to be held securely without shorting anything
- Connector clearance: USB-A plug needs ~5mm depth from shell edge
- Cap or sliding cover to protect the port
- Personalization: engraving, inlay, color
