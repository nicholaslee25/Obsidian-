## Overview

A clock or display that tracks and shows the current phase of the moon. Could be mechanical (rotating disc), electronic (OLED/e-ink), or hybrid — wooden enclosure with a circular window revealing a rotating moon disc behind it.

Hybrid is probably the best balance: physical aesthetic, accurate tracking via RTC or WiFi sync.

## Materials

- Enclosure wood (species TBD)
- Moon phase disc (printed artwork on rotating disc)
- Stepper motor to advance the disc on a 29.5-day cycle
- Microcontroller (ESP32 for WiFi sync, or RTC module for standalone)
- Optional: OLED or e-ink display panel
- Power supply
