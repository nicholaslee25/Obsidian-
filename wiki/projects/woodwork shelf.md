## Overview

A modular wooden shelf system with adjustable heights. Designed to disassemble and reassemble easily so it adapts to different spaces — expandable over time. Build one section now, add more later without starting over.

Key requirements: adjustable shelf positions, modular units, tool-free or minimal-tool disassembly.

Shelf pin holes are probably the right call for adjustable heights — drill a column of holes, use metal pins, move shelves wherever.

## Materials

- Hardwood or plywood for shelves and uprights (species TBD)
- Shelf pins / bracket hardware
- Fasteners (threaded inserts + bolts, or mortise + loose tenon for disassembly-friendly joints)
- Finish (stain, oil, or paint — TBD)
