## Overview

A handmade wooden Bluetooth speaker — custom enclosure built around quality audio components. Wood species and thickness affect resonance and tone. Sealed enclosure is easier to build; ported gives more bass.

## Materials

- Speaker driver(s) — full-range (Dayton Audio or similar)
- Bluetooth amplifier board (PAM8403, TDA7498E, or dedicated BT amp module)
- Battery cells (18650 + BMS) if portable, or AC adapter if desktop
- Wood for enclosure
- Speaker grille (fabric, metal mesh, or open)
- Wiring, terminals, power switch
- Finish
