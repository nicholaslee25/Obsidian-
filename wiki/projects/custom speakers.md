## Overview

Build a speaker system from scratch — custom chassis (wood enclosure) plus as many components as possible DIY'd. Also exploring converting dead earbuds into usable speakers.

## Approaches

- **Full build** — design and build the entire enclosure + wire drivers, crossover, etc.
- **Dead earbud conversion** — strip earbuds for drivers, build a new housing around them

Reference: [Turning dead earbuds into speakers](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNHDbXAmY_0)

## Things to Learn First

- Speaker driver specs (impedance, frequency response, sensitivity)
- Enclosure design (sealed vs. ported, cabinet tuning)
- Crossover basics (separating highs and lows to different drivers)
- Amplifier requirements
