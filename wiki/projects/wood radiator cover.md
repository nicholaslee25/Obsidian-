## Overview

A wooden radiator cover for mom. Decorative + functional — hides the radiator, acts as a shelf/surface, still allows heat to flow through.

**Gift for:** Mom
**Measurements:** TBD — need to measure radiator dimensions

## Design Considerations

- **Airflow** — must allow heat out; slatted front, perforated panel, or open grille design
- **Wood type** — needs to handle heat without warping; hardwood or properly dried softwood
- **Finish** — paint or stain? Match existing room furniture
- **Top surface** — flat top usable as a shelf/display surface
- **Access** — removable front panel or hinged for bleeding the radiator
