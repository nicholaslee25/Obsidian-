## Overview

A custom wooden nightstand or desk piece with a built-in wireless (MagSafe-compatible) charger — phone sits on the surface and charges without plugging in. Clean, minimal, handmade. Wireless coil embedded just below the wood surface (needs ~3–5mm max thickness at the charging spot for the field to pass through).

## Materials

- Wood for enclosure (species TBD)
- Wireless charging module (Qi or MagSafe coil)
- USB-C input port
- Wiring
- Optional: drawer hardware, additional storage components
- Finish
