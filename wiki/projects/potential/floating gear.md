## Overview

A gear (or gear assembly) that magnetically levitates and optionally spins — a mechanical display piece. Active levitation (electromagnet + hall effect sensor + control loop) is the most viable approach. Add a contactless coil underneath to spin it. Full conversation piece for a mechanical engineer's desk.

## Materials

- Electromagnetic levitation module
- Hall effect sensor + control circuit (or buy a complete active levitation kit)
- Gear (machined metal, 3D printed, or cast resin)
- Optional: drive coil for spinning
- Base/enclosure (wood or metal)
- Power supply
