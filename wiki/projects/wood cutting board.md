# Wood Cutting Board

**Type:** Project
**Tags:** #project #woodworking
**Last updated:** 2026-05-20

---

## Concept

Handmade wooden cutting board. Could go end grain, edge grain, or face grain depending on wood and time available.

---

## Feature Brainstorm

Things to decide / consider before building:

**Shape**
- Standard rectangle? Or something cooler — oval, paddle handle, octagonal corners
- Handle cutout for hanging

**Wood & Grain**
- End grain (hardest, most knife-friendly, checkerboard look) vs edge grain vs face grain
- Multi-wood contrast: maple + walnut + cherry is the classic combo
- Herringbone, chevron, or striped inlay patterns

**Functional Features**
- Juice groove routed around the perimeter (keeps meat/fruit juices off the counter)
- Rubber feet (non-slip — drill or epoxy four bumpers to the bottom)
- Hanging hole
- Slot for a phone/tablet to prop up while cooking

**Aesthetic Features**
- Contrasting wood inlay (geometric pattern, logo, initials)
- Pyrography or CNC-engraved design
- Personalization (name, date, phrase)

**Finish**
- Food-safe oil: mineral oil is cheapest; butcher block conditioner (beeswax + mineral oil) is better long-term
- No polyurethane on food contact surfaces

**Open Questions**
- [ ] End grain or edge grain?
- [ ] Single wood or multi-wood?
- [ ] Juice groove — yes or no?
- [ ] Gift for someone or keep?
