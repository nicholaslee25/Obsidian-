## Overview

A lightbulb that magnetically levitates and glows — powered wirelessly through the magnetic field while floating in mid-air. Electromagnetic levitation module holds the bulb suspended; inductive coil powers the LED filament through the air. A feedback loop keeps it stable.

Easiest approach: buy levitation + wireless charging modules (~$15–30) and build a custom base/enclosure around them.

## Materials

- Electromagnetic levitation module (with wireless power transfer)
- LED filament bulb (compatible with the module)
- Base material (wood, metal, or concrete)
- Power supply (usually 12V DC)
- Wiring
